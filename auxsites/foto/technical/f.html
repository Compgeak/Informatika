Focal Lenght & Field of View

Focal length determines the field of view and the size of the lens. A longer focal length will call for a longer lens (with exception of mirror based lenses) and will give us a narrower field of view. This will make the subject appear more close up and is useful for photographying subjects that are small and far away.

 

A longer focal length will also mean there will be less difference in size change with distance.
