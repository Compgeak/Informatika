The Rise of Digital Photography

Ever since the invention of the digital optical sensor, photography has been tightly involved with digital technology. By now digital cameras have become mainstream and analog cameras are rarely ever still seen.

Digital cameras have enabled us to more easily create, manipulate and share our photos. This is the main reason digital photography has become so widespread.
